What Is This Platform?
Core Concept
This is a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform built around a machine-learning API for retail and e-commerce businesses. Instead of hiring a data scientist or building your own pipeline, you simply upload a CSV of your monthly sales history and the platform does the rest — training, optimizing, and storing a dedicated model just for you.
🎯
Per-User Model Isolation
Each account trains and owns its own model. Your data never touches another user's model.
📊
Multi-Product Forecasting
Upload data for hundreds of products at once — the model learns each product's unique sales pattern.
🔄
Train Once, Forecast Many
Train the model once with your historical data, then call /forecast as many times as you need — instantly.
💾
Persistent Model Storage
Your trained model is saved on the server and survives restarts — no need to retrain unless you want to.

How the ML Works The platform trains two models simultaneously — XGBoost (gradient-boosted trees) and Ridge Regression (regularized linear model) — using engineered lag features (past N months of sales) and rolling averages. Both are evaluated by RMSE on a held-out validation set, and the best-performing model is automatically selected and saved for your account.
