Step 1 — Sign Up & Login
Auth Portal
1
Open the Login page
Open login.html in your browser — you will see two tabs: Login and Sign Up.
2
Create a new account
Click the Sign Up tab — enter your email, password, and confirmation. A verification email will be sent; open it and click the link.
3
Login and get your token
Switch to the Login tab and enter your credentials. After login you will be redirected automatically to Swagger and your token will be saved.
4
Confirm authorization in Swagger
In Swagger click the Authorize 🔒 button at the top — your token is pre-filled automatically. Click Authorize then Close.
📋
Step 2 — CSV Format
Data Prep
Your CSV file must contain exactly these columns:
month
Month date — e.g. 2024-01-01
product_id
Product identifier — e.g. P001
number_of_product_purchases
Actual sales count for that month
number_of_times_added_to_cart
Number of times the product was added to cart
number_of_times_add_followed_by_purchase
Cart additions that resulted in a purchase
number_of_times_add_followed_by_no_purchase
Cart additions that did not result in a purchase
example.csv
month,product_id,number_of_product_purchases,number_of_times_added_to_cart,...
2024-01-01,P001,120,340,98,242
2024-02-01,P001,135,380,110,270
2024-01-01,P002,88,210,72,138
⚠️
Important Each product needs data for several months so the model can learn meaningful patterns.
🏋️
Step 3 — POST /train
POST · Once
Upload your full historical CSV here — the model trains on it and is saved on the server, linked exclusively to your account. You only need to do this once unless you have new, different data.
In Swagger
1
Open the /train endpoint
In Swagger find POST /train and click on it.
2
Click "Try it out"
An Upload File button will appear — select your CSV file.
3
Click Execute
Wait a moment — training time depends on your data size. When done you will see a response with model metrics.
Response — JSON
{
  "message":       "✅ Model trained and saved successfully",
  "model_name":    "XGBoost",
  "train_rmse":    0.9234,
  "val_rmse":      1.4970,
  "baseline_rmse": 18.1430,
  "best_lags":     3,
  "best_roll":     3
}
💡
val_rmseThe lower the value, the more accurate the model's predictions.
💾
Persisted on serverIf you restart the server your model will still be there — no need to retrain.
🚀
Step 4 — POST /forecast
POST · Anytime
Once you have run /train once, you can use /forecast anytime at full speed — no retraining involved. Upload a CSV with your current data and get a two-month prediction instantly.
In Swagger
1
Open the /forecast endpoint
In Swagger find POST /forecast and click "Try it out".
2
Upload your CSV
You can upload the same historical CSV or a newer one — the format is identical.
3
Click Execute and download the result
A CSV file containing the two-month forecast for each product will be returned. Click Download file in Swagger.
Response — CSV Download
product_id,forecast_month,predicted_purchases
P001,2025-01,142.50
P001,2025-02,158.30
P002,2025-01,96.00
P002,2025-02,103.75
⚠️
If you get a 404 error It means you have not run /train yet — go back to Step 3 first.
🔄
Retrain — DELETE /model
DELETE · Optional
If you have new data or want to retrain from scratch — delete the saved model first, then upload the new data to /train.
1
Open DELETE /model in Swagger
Click "Try it out" then Execute — this deletes only your saved model, no one else's.
2
Go back to POST /train
Upload your new CSV and the model will be retrained from scratch on the fresh data.
📊
GET /metrics — Check Model Performance
GET
At any time you can view the performance metrics of your saved model without uploading any file.
📉
val_rmseAverage error on validation data — the lower the better.
📏
baseline_rmseError with no model at all — shows how much better your model is vs. a naive baseline.
🎯
train_rmseError on training data — if much lower than val_rmse, the model may be overfitting.
🔢
best_lags / best_rollNumber of past months the model relied on during training.
